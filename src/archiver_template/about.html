Welcome to my website! Allow me to introduce you to my latest project, a client designed to securely store and archive all the posts from a Skyblog. As many of you may know, Skyblog is an online blogging platform that will be discontinued in the near future. With this impending closure, valuable memories and cherished moments risk being lost forever.

Driven by the desire to preserve those precious posts and ensure they are not forgotten, I embarked on this endeavor to create a client that enables users to save their Skyblog content locally and securely. This client serves as a safeguard, offering a reliable way to retain and cherish those memories for years to come.

With the help of this client, users can effortlessly download and store their entire Skyblog archive, including posts and images. The process is simple, yet powerful, as it allows individuals to maintain full control over their cherished content.
